GAME: Breaking Bad

Walkthrough

Game Description

Help the Walter White cross the road in Breaking Bad. Breaking Bad is an original game inspired by the series Breaking Bad and has similar gameplay to Crossy Road. For those of you who have never played Crossy Roads all you need to do is cross a busy road bustling with dangerous traffic.

Here one must look at the speed of the traffic and move as quickly as they can also ensure to avoid being smashed by the cars. The game is endless and you must try and cross as many highways as possible and register a high score.
